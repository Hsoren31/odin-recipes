Family Lasagna Recipe

Our Family Lasagna

This is our family lasagna that has been passed down from my grandma Jan. Traditionally, we only make it during Halloween and on my birthday, making it a real treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Lasagna Pasta
  • 1 1/2 lb. Ground Beef
  • 2 lbs. Ricotta or Cottage Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2 Jars Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 lb. Mozzarella Cheese, Thinly Sliced
