Family Lasagna Recipe

Our Family Lasagna

This is our family lasagna that has been passed down from my grandma Jan. Traditionally, we only make it during Halloween and on my birthday, making it a real treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Lasagna Pasta
  • 1 1/2 lb. Ground Beef
  • 2 lbs. Ricotta or Cottage Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2 Jars Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 lb. Mozzarella Cheese, Thinly Sliced

Steps

  1. Boil 6 quarts water. Add 1 tsp. Salt and 1 tbsp. oil. Cook Lasagna pasta at a boil for about 15 minutes. Drain and cover with cold water.
  2. Brown onions and ground beef chuck until crumbly. Drain excess fat. Stir in Spaghetti Sauce
  3. Spoon 1/2 cup of the mixture into the bottom of a 13x9x2 inch baking pan. Drain noodles. Layer noodles,ricotta, Mozzarella cheese, meat sauce and parmesan cheese.
  4. End with a layer of noodles and meat sauce. Sprinkle top with parmesan. Cover with greased foil.
  5. Bake in a preheated moderate oven (375 degrees F) for 1 hour.
  6. Let stand for 15 minutes and then cut into 12 pieces. Remove pieces with a spatula.
