Homemade Chili Recipe

Easy Crockpot Chili

This recipe is perfect for the winter, cause it'll warm you up with the perfect amount of spice.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 28 oz. stewed tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp. pepper
  • 2-4 cloves garlic
  • 28 oz. chili beans
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2-3 tsp. chili powder
  • salt to taste
